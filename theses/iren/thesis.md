## Core Thesis

### What I Believe

IREN is executing a credible pivot from Bitcoin miner to vertically integrated AI cloud infrastructure provider — and the market is still pricing it closer to a crypto play than a hyperscaler-feeder. The company owns its data centers outright (>80% US-based, renewable power), operates with low-cost power economics, and has now secured NVIDIA as both a $3.4B AI cloud customer and a potential $2.1B equity partner via warrants at $70/share. That combination is structurally different from spec-stage neo-clouds.

The insight the market is underweighting: IREN's owned-infrastructure model creates a cost moat that rental-model competitors (CoreWeave, Lambda) cannot easily replicate. When NVIDIA designs DSX AI factories around IREN's sites at 5GW scale, it is effectively certifying IREN's infrastructure stack and locking in long-duration demand at the same time. The warrant structure ($70 strike) also tells you what NVIDIA thinks this stock is worth over a 5-year horizon.

Q3 FY2026 was a deliberate miss — BTC price weakness hit mining revenue while AI cloud was still ramping. The underlying AI cloud business trend (H1 FY2026: +152% YoY total revenue) remains intact. The question is whether the AI cloud segment can scale fast enough at Sweetwater to offset mining volatility. The NVIDIA contract makes that question much easier to answer.

### Why I Believe It (Evidence)

**Moat & competitive position**
1. Owns proprietary data centers and electrical infrastructure; >80% US capacity; renewable energy underpins low power cost. Source: IREN 10-K filed 2025-08-28 → business/chunk0
   > *…data center ownership also allows our business to benefit from more sustainable cash flows and operational flexibility in comparison with operators that rely upon third-party hosting services or short-term land leases…*

2. AI Cloud Services launched 2024; GPU-based cloud compute sold to AI training and inference customers; model is direct ownership of servers. Source: IREN 10-K filed 2025-08-28 → business/chunk28
   > *…AI Cloud Services, launched in 2024, that generates revenue by providing access to cloud-based GPU computing to customers for AI training and inference…*

3. Business model is explicitly diversifying away from pure Bitcoin mining into HPC and AI services to generate more stable, recurring revenue. Source: IREN 10-K filed 2025-08-28 → business/chunk1
   > *…diversifying our revenue sources into HPC and AI services, which includes the provision of AI Cloud Services and potentially Colocation Services…*

**NVIDIA partnership (post-Q3, May 2026)**
4. NVIDIA committed to deploy up to 5 gigawatts of DSX-aligned AI infrastructure across IREN's global data center pipeline — $3.4B AI cloud contract plus warrants for up to 30M shares at $70. Source: NVIDIA Newsroom 2026-05-07, IREN 8-K filed 2026-05-08
   > *…NVIDIA and IREN intend to support deployment of up to 5 gigawatts of NVIDIA DSX-aligned AI infrastructure across IREN's global data center pipeline over time…*

**Financial trajectory**
5. H1 FY2026 total revenue: $425.0M, +152% YoY; dual-segment reporting (AI Cloud Services + Bitcoin Mining) adopted Q2 FY2026. Source: iren-brief.json (derived from 10-Q filed 2026-01-30)

7. GPU fleet ~99,900 units installed or on order (H200, B200, B300, GB300) as of Dec 2025; 46 EH/s hashrate; zero BTC on balance sheet (daily liquidation). Source: IREN 10-Q filed 2026-01-30

**Capital structure risks**
8. ATM Sales Agreement in place; Prepaid Forward Transactions for dilution management; B. Riley committed equity facility. Dilution is structural — manage with position sizing. Source: IREN 10-K filed 2025-08-28 → business/chunk23
